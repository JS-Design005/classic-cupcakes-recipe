Classic Cupcakes

Prep: 15 minutes | Cooking 10 minutes | Makes 12 cupcakes

Nothing in a baker's book gets more classic than grandma's mini Classic Cupcakes! With vanilia flavoured cake, topped with coloured icing and finished with a pinch of sprinkles. This recipie has is a must learn for all bakers and perfect for teaching children the basics!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-raising flour, sifted
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 125g butter, melted, cooled
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • Sprinkles, to decorate icing
  • 1 1/2 cups pure icing sugar
  • 1-1 1/2 tbsp water
  • Food colouring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200C or 180C fan-forced. Grease a 12 x 1/2-cup capacity muffin pan. Alternatively, line holes with paper cases. Combine flour and caster sugar in a bowl. Make a well in the centre.
  2. Add milk, butter, eggs and vanilla to flour mixture. Using a metal spoon, stir gently to combine.
  3. Spoon mixture into prepared muffin pan. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Stand in pan for 5 minutes before transfering to a wire rack to cool.
  4. Make icing: Sift icing sugar into a bowl. Add food colouring and water. Stir until smooth and well combined. Spoon icing over cupcakes. Decorate with sprinkles.
