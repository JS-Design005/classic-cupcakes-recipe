Classic Cupcakes

Prep: 15 minutes | Cooking: 10 minutes | Makes: 12 cupcakes

Nothing in a baker's book gets more classic than Grandma's mini Classic Cupcakes! Made with vanilia flavoured cake, topped with coloured icing and finished with a pinch of sprinkles. This recipie has is a must learn for all bakers and perfect for teaching children the basics!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sifted self-raising flour
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar, 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 125g melted and cooled butter
  • 1-1 1/2 tbsp water, 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups pure icing sugar
  • Sprinkles & food colouring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your fan-forced oven between 200C - 180C. Grease, or line your 12 x 1/2-cup capacity muffin pan holes with paper cases. In a bowl, combine flour and caster sugar. When it is well mixed, create a small well in the centre where we will add out wet ingredients.
  2. Add the milk, butter, eggs and vanilla into the well that we made in our dry ingredients. Using a non-stick spoon, gently stir to combine.
  3. Scoup mixture into the lined pan holes and Bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Keep in mind that the batter will rise a little, so ad a little less that you'd expect! Leave to sit in pan for 5 minutes before transfering the cupcakes to a wire cooling rack.
  4. Now we can make the icing! Sift the icing sugar into a bowl and add water and some food colouring. Stir the mixture until it is the same colour and smooth. Scoup the icing and spread over the top of the cupcakes. Finish off with some sprinkles!
Allergen Note:

Contains: Gluten (Wheat), Egg, and Milk. May Contain: Soy and Tree Nuts (sprinkles).
